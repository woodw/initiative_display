Map Code

Welcome. Hover your mouse over a cell. Press a key to draw. Enjoy

  • e: empty cell
  • o: open cell floor
  • c: closed cell floor
  • f: next cell floor
  • ^: next cell north wall
  • >: next cell east wall
  • v: open cell south wall
  • <: open cell west wall
  • h: hide cell
  • u: unhide cell
  • click: toggle cell hidden value
